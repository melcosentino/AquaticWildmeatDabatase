Short-finned pilot whale (Globicephala macrorhynchus)

Short-finned pilot whales can be found in all temperate and warm waters around the world. They belong to the Delphinidae family yet their size and behaviour differs from other oceanic dolphins. They are black except for a greyish area in the ventral part. They is a conspicuous sexual dimorphism, with males reaching 5.5 m in length while females only reach 3.7 m. They live in matrilineal groups of up to 30 individuals, spending most of their time together. IUCN = Least Concern. Learn more about short-finned pilot whales here.
