Short-beaked common dolphin (Delphinus delphins)

Common dolphins are widely distributed, found in temperate and warm waters around the globe, except the Indian Ocean, found in coastal and offshore waters. Common dolphins live and travel in large groups that regularly exceed 50 individuals but can reach thousands of animals. They are highly vocal, producing a wide range of sonic and ultrasonic sounds that they use for foraging and communication purposes. IUCN = Least Concern.
