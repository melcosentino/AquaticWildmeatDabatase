Commerson's dolphin (Cephalorhynchus commersonii)

There are two subspecies of Commerson's dolphin. C. c. commersonii lives exclusively along the Southern coast of Argentina and Chile and the Falkland Islands, while C. c. kerguelenensis are found around and French Southern and Antarctic Lands. They are mostly white, while the head and pectoral fins, the, dorsal fin and tail fluke are black. They are small, reaching 1.7m in length and travel in groups of fewer than 10 individuals.
